## Description of Service

Listen2 is a text-to-speech application that converts PDFs, EPUBs, and text documents into spoken audio using neural TTS voices. The App:

- Processes all documents locally on your device
- Provides text-to-speech playback with word/sentence highlighting
- Allows downloading of additional voice models
- Works primarily offline (network only required for voice downloads)
