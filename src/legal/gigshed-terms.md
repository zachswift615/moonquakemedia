## Description of Service

GigShed is an on-device practice tool for musicians, for iPhone and iPad. The App helps you learn songs you already own by:

- Splitting a recording into separate stems (vocals, drums, bass, and other)
- Muting, soloing, and mixing those stems
- Changing the key and playback speed independently
- Looping sections and following a detected beat grid with a metronome
- Generating and editing a chords-and-lyrics chart
- Exporting your mix and charts, and handing charts off to the companion app, GigScroll

All processing runs locally on your device. The App works offline and stores your data on your device.
