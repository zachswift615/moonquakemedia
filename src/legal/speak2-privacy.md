## Introduction

Moonquake Media ("we", "our", or "us") operates the Speak2 application (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our App.

We are committed to protecting your privacy. Speak2 is designed as a privacy-first application that processes everything locally on your Mac.

## Information We Collect

### Personal Data

**We do not collect any personal data.**

Speak2 does not require an account, email address, or any personal information to use.

### Voice and Audio Data

**All audio processing happens entirely on your device.**

- Audio is recorded only while you actively hold the dictation key
- Audio is processed in memory and is never written to disk
- Audio is never transmitted to any server or third party
- No transcription history is stored

### Usage Data

**We do not collect any usage data.**

Speak2 contains no analytics, no telemetry, and no crash reporting. We have no way to know how you use the app.

## Third-Party Services

Speak2 does not integrate with any third-party services. The speech recognition model is downloaded once during setup and runs entirely on your device thereafter.

## Data Storage

All app settings and your pronunciation dictionary are stored locally on your Mac using standard macOS preferences. This data is not synced, uploaded, or shared.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.
